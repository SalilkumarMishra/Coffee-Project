Coffee Sourcing

Direct Trade Relationships

We believe in building direct relationships with coffee farmers around the world. This approach ensures:

  • Fair Wages: Farmers receive above-market prices for their exceptional beans
  • Quality Control: We work closely with farmers to ensure the highest quality standards
  • Sustainability: Supporting environmentally responsible farming practices
  • Transparency: Knowing exactly where your coffee comes from

Our beans are sourced from regions including Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Indonesia, each bringing unique flavor profiles to our selection.
